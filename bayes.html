Summary of this video by 3Blue1Brown.

We're trying to work out the probability someone is a librarian given they look like one - P(H|E), in layman's terms, the probabilities we need to construct the diagram below are:

  • The percentage of librarians in the whole population - P(H)
  • The percentage of librarians that look like librarians - P(E|H)
  • The percentage of non-librarians that look like librarians - P(E|¬H)
